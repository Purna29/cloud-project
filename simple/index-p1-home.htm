ICC TOURNMENTS

International Cricket Council is the global governing body of cricket.It organises world championship events such as Cricket World Cup, Women's Cricket World Cup, ICC T20 World Cup, ICC Women's T20 World Cup,ICC Champions Thropy and Under-19 Cricket World Cup.Team India participate all the events organised by the ICC.Indian Cricket Team become a member in ICC in the year 1926.We won the 1983 Cricket World cup under the captaincy of Kapil Dev and again in 2011 after 28 Years we won Cricket World Cup under the captaincy of Mahendra Singh Dhoni,under him we won T20 Mens T20 World cup in 2007 and Champions Thropy in 2013 in England.

INTERNATIONAL LEVEL

In International Level, team India plays against 12 ICC full members in different format types like ODI,Test and T20. ICC give ranks in every format to every country team based on their performance in each format. We played the particilar series against another country team either in home country or away.There is series called Tri- Series which involued 3 country teams.Team India have 2 Tri-Series in every year one is India-Pakistan-Bangladesh and the other one is India-Sri Lanka-Australia.

DOMESTIC LEVEL

The domestic competitions include the Ranji Trophy, the Duleep Trophy, the Vijay Hazare Trophy, the Deodhar Trophy, the Irani Trophy and the NKP Salve Challenger Trophy. In addition, the BCCI conducts the Indian Premier League, a Twenty20 competition, which is also one of the biggest sports leagues in the world and the biggest cricket league in the world. The Indian cricket team is also accredited with the honour of winning all the ICC tournaments under M.S. Dhoni's captaincy, which is a world record.
